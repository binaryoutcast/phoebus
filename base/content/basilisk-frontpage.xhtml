Basilisk Add-ons Site

What are Add-ons?

Add-ons let you customize Basilisk with extra functionality or a different look.

There are several types of add-ons. Extensions expand the capabilities of the browser, themes allow you to personalize Basilisk's visual aesthetic, and plugins further extend the browser with specialized external components such as the Adobe Flash Player.
